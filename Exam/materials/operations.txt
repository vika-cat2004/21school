Operations can be: unary, binary, and ternary.

1. Operation of assignment (=, compound assignment: +=,-=,*=,/=,%=, &=, |=, etc...)
2. Arithmetic operations (unary: +,-, binary: +,-,/,*,%)
3. Comparison operations (==,!=,<,>,<=,>=)
4. Logical operations (unary: !, binary: &&, ||)
5. Bitwise operations (unary: ^, binary: &,|,~,<<,>>)
